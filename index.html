$$\delta_j^L = \frac{\partial C}{\partial z^L_j}$$
$$= \frac{\partial C}{\partial a^L_j} \frac{\partial a^L_j}{\partial z^L_j}$$
$$= \frac{\partial}{\partial a^L_j} \frac{1}{2} (y_j - a^L_j)^2 \cdot \frac{\partial}{\partial z^L_j} \sigma(z^L_j)$$
$$= (a^L_j - y_j) \cdot \sigma'(z^L_j)$$
